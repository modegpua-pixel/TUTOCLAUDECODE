Where automation can already take work off your plate

Pick the friction that looks most like your daily routine. In 20 minutes, we check your tools, your data and the first useful version.

Handle incoming requests

Friction

Tickets, emails and messages arrive while the team is already busy. Customers wait, follow up, or get frustrated.

● Automation

The workflow qualifies the request, gathers missing info, drafts the reply and routes urgent topics to the right person.

⇄↗

Sort & route tickets

Friction

Every request has to be read, categorized and assigned by hand, which slows down the first response.

● Automation

Sorting and assignment happen automatically based on your rules, for a much shorter first-response time.

↻↗

Follow up without thinking

Friction

Renewals, payments, incomplete files: follow-ups matter, but they always come after the fires of the day.

● Automation

The system spots what's waiting for action, sends the right follow-up and keeps a clear trace in your tools.

◎↗

Onboard new customers

Friction

Every new customer needs the same welcome steps, often done manually and sometimes forgotten.

● Automation

Onboarding sequences trigger automatically and track each customer's progress, without missing a step.

▤↗

Keep the CRM up to date

Friction

After conversations, notes stay in everyone's head. The CRM becomes incomplete and unreliable.

● Automation

The key information from conversations is captured and synced automatically into your CRM.

▥↗

Prepare reporting

Friction

Support numbers are scattered across several tools. Reporting takes time and arrives late.

● Automation

Metrics are consolidated automatically for clear steering, with no manual re-entry or export.
